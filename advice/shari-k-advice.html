Hi, my name is Shari and I have some advice for you!

Consistency is Key!

Nothing ever happens by itself, right? So if you want to make a change and learn something new, of course it will take some time! The best way to ensure that is to set a time and place for regular practice. It works for piano (or knitting, juggling, whatever is your thing...), right?

To keep it up, though, you need to be able to tolerate hitting the wrong notes sometimes. You may have to fiddle with things a bit to see what isn't working. Don't worry, a LOT of resources are available to get you back in tune.

Be fearless -- and don't expect to know what you are doing.

This is one of those "fake it till you make it" experiences. I mean, we learn new skills by doing them. That means using code we don't really "understand" at first, so that later it will be familiar -- and understandable!

Have fun!

Learning new things and enjoying the experience is hard-wired into human beings. At least, the joy of succeeding is. Celebrate every little victory -- we've got this!
